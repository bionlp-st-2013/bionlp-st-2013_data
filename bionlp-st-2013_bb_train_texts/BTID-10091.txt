Borrelia garinii PBi
Description
Borrelia garinii. This organism was orginally classified as Borrelia burgdorferi, however B. garinii was subsequently determined to be a separate species based on genetic analysis. The type strain of B. garinii was isolated from Ixodes ricinus in France. B. garinii is a major causative agent of tick-borne borreliosis in Europe. Neurologic symptoms, such as arthritis, meningitis, and extreme leg and back pain are characteristic of infection by B. garinii.

Borrelia garinii s train PBi. This strain (OspA serotype 4) was isolated in Germany from the cerebrospinal fluid of a patient with neuroborreliosis.
